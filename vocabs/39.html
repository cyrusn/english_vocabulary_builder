396 conservative /kǝnˈsǝːvǝtiv/

- His conservative views made him unpopular.		 a. 守的，守舊的

- The Conservative Party is now in power.		 a. 大寫) (英國，加拿大等) 保守黨的
例句 現在由保守黨執政。

- The conservatives are opposed to any change.		 n. 守者，守舊者
例句 保守分子反對變革。
