Vocabulary 600

# Title Definition
341 exact /iɡˈzækt/

- It was difficult to tell her exact age.		 a. 切的，精確的
例句 很難說出她的確實年齡。

- The scholar is an exact thinker.		 a. 確無誤的，精密的
例句 這位學者思維嚴謹。

- The kidnappers exacted ransoms for their hostages.		 vt. 要；勒索
例句 綁匪勒索人質的贖金。
342 investigate /inˈvеstiɡеit/

- The detective was sent to investigate the crime.		 vt. 調查，研究
例句 那位偵探被派去調查該罪行。

- The principal promised to investigate into that affair.		 vi. 調查，研究 [（+into）]
例句 校長答應去調查那件事情。
343 resolution /ˌrеzǝˈluːʃǝn/

- Worst of all, he had no hope of shaking her resolution.		 n. 心；決定 [C] [+to-v]
例句 最糟的是，他知道無法動搖她的決心。

- We have passed a resolution to build a new laboratory.		 n. 會議等的) 正式決定，決議 [C] [+to-v] [+that]
例句 我們已通過決議建一個新實驗室。

- His generosity led to the resolution of all our difficulties.		 n. 決；解答；解除 [U] [（+of）]
例句 他的慷慨相助使我們的困難全都得以解決。

- He is a man of high principle.		 n. 義，節操 [U]
例句 他是節操高尚的人。

- Her brothers are both career diplomats.		 a. 職業的，專業的 [B]

346 ensure /inˈʃuǝ/

- I can't ensure his being on time.
- I can't ensure that he will be there on time.
- This medicine will ensure you a good night's sleep.		 vt. 證；擔保 [O9] [+that] [+v-ing]
例句 我不能保證他的準時。
例句 我不能擔保他會及時到那兒。
例句 這藥將保證你一晚睡眠良好。

- These are safety devices to ensure workers against accidents.		 vt. 安全，保護 [Q] [（+from/against）]
例句 這些安全設施是為了保護工人不出事故。

- Buddhism is the religion founded by Buddha in North India.		 n. 教 [U]

- He is a man without religion.		 n. 教信仰；宗教生活，修道生活 [U]

例句 足球比賽他每場必看。
348 source /sɒːs/

- Do you know the source of Amazon River?		 n. 河的) 源頭；水源

- They are required to publish the sources of their campaign funds.		 n. 源；來源 [（+of）]
例句 他們必須公布自己競選經費的來源。

- The news comes from a reliable source.		 n. 供消息（或證據）者；消息（或證據）來源 [P1]

- They sourced the spoke nuts from our company.		 vt. 獲得（商品、零件）

- They are sourcing from abroad in order to save money.		 vi. 從其他公司、國家購得零件、材料等；開源
例句 為了省錢，他們的材料、零件從國外開源。

- The offender was sent to a correctional facility.		 n. 供特定用途的) 場所 [C] [（+for）]
