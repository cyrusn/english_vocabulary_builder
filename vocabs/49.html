- Jennie made a great effort to recover herself.
- She has recovered her health.		 vt. 復；使恢復原狀
例句 珍妮努力使自己鎮定下來。
例句 她已恢復了健康。

- It's hard to recover lost time.		 vt. 回，彌補
例句 彌補失去的時間並不容易。

- It took a long time for him to recover from a bad cold.
- I think she will recover.		 vi. 復健康；恢復原狀；恢復 [（+from）]
例句 他患重感冒，很長時間才康復。
例句 我想她會痊癒的。

493 remark /riˈmɑːk/

- I remarked the tense atmosphere as soon as I entered the room.		 vt. 意，看到；覺察

- Prof. Smith remarked on the difference between the two dictionaries.		 vi. 談論，議論；評論 [（+on/upon）]

- He had a habit of making humorous remarks.		 n. 辭；談論，評論 [C] [（+on/upon/about/at）]
例句 他有說幽默話的習慣。

- He saw nothing worthy of remark at the exhibition.		 n. 意；察覺 [U]
例句 他在展覽會上沒有看到值得注目的東西。
