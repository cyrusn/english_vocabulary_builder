# Title Definition
421 commission /kǝˈmiʃǝn/

- He gets a 10% commission on everything he sells.		 n. 金 [C] [U] [（+on）]
例句 他從銷售的每件商品中得到百分之十的佣金。

- We all approve of the commission of authority to him.		 n. 權限，任務等的) 委任，委託 [U]
例句 我們都贊成把職權委任於他。

- The problem was assigned to a commission.		 n. 員會 [C] [G]
例句 這個問題交給一個委員會處理。

- The artist was commissioned to paint a picture for the hall.		 vt. 任，委託 [O2]
例句 這位藝術家受委託為這個大廳作一幅畫。

- He was commissioned a general at the age of 50.		 vt. 命，授銜 [O1]
例句 他五十歲時被任命為將軍。

- The book made a great impact on its readers.		 n. 響；作用 [S1] [（+on）]

424 feature /fiːtʃǝ/

- This is a key feature of our society.		 n. 徵，特色 [（+of）]
例句 這是我們社會的一個主要特點。

- He could recall her features very distinctly.		 n. 貌，相貌 [P]

- The local newspaper ran a feature on child labor.		 n. 報紙等的) 特寫，特別報導，專欄

- The new movie features two of my favorite actors.		 vt. 電影) 由……主演

- The museum's exhibit features paintings of colonial times.		 vt. 載，以……作為號召
例句 博物館的展覽品特別介紹殖民時代的繪畫。

- Economic issues featured very largely in the Prime Minister's speech.		 vi. 起重要作用；作為主要角色 [（+in）]
例句 首相的講話中很大一部分涉及經濟問題。
