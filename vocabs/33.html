# Title Definition
331 damage /ˈdæmidʒ/

- She has not got any damages.		 n. 償金 [P]
例句 她沒有得到任何賠償費。

- A torrent of rain came down and damaged the crops.		 vt. 損害，毀壞
例句 大雨傾盆而下，損害了農作物。

- We referred the proposal to the board of directors.		 vt. ……提交，交付 [（+to）]
