# Title Definition
471 trend /trеnd/

- The mountains have a western trend.		 n. 海岸、河流等的) 走向
例句 群山向西延伸。

- The current trend is towards informal clothing.		 n. 勢，傾向；時尚
例句 目前的趨勢是穿著比較隨便。

- The road trends to the north.		 vi. 向；折向，轉向
例句 這條路是向北的。

- Share prices have been trending down.		 vi. 向，傾向
例句 股票價格一直趨向下降。
472 bother /ˈbɒðǝ/

- I can't bother him with my little affairs.		 vt. 擾，打攪
例句 我不能因自己那點小事去打擾他。

- The way my brother talked to Mother bothered me.		 vt. 惱怒
例句 我兄弟對母親說話的那副模樣惹我惱火。

- The complexities of interpersonal relations bothered her.		 vt. 困惑，使不安
例句 人際關係的複雜情況使她困惑不已。

- Don't bother about that trifling matter.		 vi. 惱，擔心 [（+with/about）]
例句 別為那件小事煩惱。

- You needn't bother to come to my office.		 vi. 煩，費心 [（+with/about）] [+to-v] [+v-ing]
例句 你不必特地來我辦公室。

- We had a lot of bother finding a vacant apartment.		 n. 1 煩惱，麻煩 [U]
例句 我們費了不少周折才找到一間空的公寓。

- Tom is a bother to his teacher.		 n. 人煩惱的人（或事物） [S]
例句 湯姆使他老師大傷腦筋。
473 regret /riˈɡrеt/

- I regret that you see it like that.
- I believe you will regret leaving Paris.
- I regret the loss of her friendship.		 vt. 悔；因……而遺憾 [+v-ing] [+that]

例句 我相信你會為離開巴黎而後悔的。
例句 我為失去她的友誼而遺憾。

- I regret his death.		 vt. 惜；哀悼；思念
例句 我對他的逝世痛惜不已。

- I regret to say that I am unable to help you.		 vt. ……抱歉，遺憾 [+to-v] [+that]
例句 很抱歉，我愛莫能助。

- Those who have not bought insurance are now regretting.		 vi. 感到後悔；感到抱歉；遺憾
例句 那些沒有買保險的人現在後悔了。

- Shelley had no regrets for his actions.
- He felt no shame and no regret.		 n. 悔，悔恨；抱歉，遺憾 [U] [C] [（+at/for）]
例句 雪萊對他的行為一點都不感到後悔。
例句 他既不感到羞愧也不感到遺憾。

- He expressed regret over the death of your father.		 n. 悼，悲歎 [U] [（+over）]
例句 他對你父親的逝世表示哀悼。

- Tommy sent his regrets.
- Please accept my regrets at having to refuse.		 n. 示謝絕的短柬 [P]
例句 湯米寄來了他的謝絕函。
例句 懇辭，謹致歉意。

477 stake /stеik/

- He put up a stake to support the newly planted tree.		 n. ，標樁；棍子，棒 [C]

- He planted and staked all the new trees.		 vt. ……於樁上；以樁支撐 [（+up）]

- The gold miner staked out his claim.		 vt. 樁標出的界限 [（+out/off）]
