Vocabulary 600

# Title Definition
591 yield /jiːld/

- These apple trees yield plenty of fruit this year.		 vt. 產；結出（果實）；產生（效果，收益等）
例句 這些蘋果樹今年結了許多蘋果。

- He was forced to yield the castle.		 vt. 迫放棄
例句 他被迫放棄城堡。

- He didn't yield himself to his rival.		 vt. 屈服，使投降
例句 他沒有向對手屈服。

- These fruit trees yielded poorly last year.		 vi. 產 [Q]
例句 去年這些果樹產量很低。

- We will never yield to invaders.		 vi. 從；屈服；投降 [（+to）]
例句 我們絕不會向侵略者屈服。

- The current yield on municipal bonds is 10%.		 n. 產量；收穫量；收益，利潤 [C] [U]
例句 當前市政債券的利潤是百分之十。

- Why don't you inquire by telephone?		 vi. 詢問；調查 [（+about/into）]
例句 你為何不打電話詢問？
594 deposit /diˈpɒzit/

- You can deposit your bags at the counter.		 vt. 下，放置；寄存 [O]

- The river deposited silt at its mouth.		 vt. 沉澱；使沉積
例句 那條河在河口沉積淤泥。

- He deposited 5,000 dollars in the bank.		 vt. （錢）儲存，存放（銀行等） [（+in）]
例句 他在銀行存了五千元。

- You must pay a deposit if you want to reserve the room.		 n. 證金；押金；定金 [C] [S1]
例句 你要預訂房間，就得先付定金。

- The country is short of known deposits of coal.		 n. 澱物；礦床 [C] [U]
例句 這個國家缺少已知的煤礦礦床。
595 afford /ǝˈfɒːd/

- They did not consider whether they could afford the time or not.
- We can't afford to pay such a price.		 vt. 常與can, could, be able to連用) 買得起；有足夠的……（去做……） [+to-v]
例句 他們沒有考慮是否抽得出時間。
例句 我們付不起這個價錢。

- Dancing affords us pleasure.		 vt. 書面語】 提供，給予 [O1]
例句 跳舞給我們帶來快樂。
596 convince /kǝnˈvins/

- He convinced me of his innocence.

597 shame /ʃеim/

- She felt great shame at having failed the exam.		 n. 恥（心），羞愧（感） [U] [（+at）]

- Your bad behavior brings shame on the whole school.		 n. 辱，恥辱 [U] [（+on）]

- It's a shame I haven't heard from you for years.		 n. 事；倒霉的事；令人憎惡的事 [S]
例句 真遺憾，我多年沒有收到你的信了。

- She was shamed and frightened.		 vt. 感到羞恥 [（+for）]
例句 她既羞愧又驚慌。

- He was shamed into admitting his guilt.		 vt. 感羞愧而…… [（+into/out of）]

- Her academic achievements shamed her brothers.		 vt. 過，使相形見絀
例句 她的學術成就使她的兄弟們相形見絀。
