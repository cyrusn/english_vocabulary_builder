# Title Definition
51 board /bɒːd/

- He sawed the board in half.		 n. 板；板 [C]
例句 他將木板鋸成兩塊。

- We will provide room and board for them.		 n. 食；伙食 [U]
例句 我們將提供他們的食宿。

- He has recently joined the board of the company.		 n. 員會；理事會；董事會；(政府的) 部，局，會 [C] [G]
例句 他最近加入了該公司的董事會。

- He boarded up the windows.		 vt. 木板覆蓋（或封閉） [（+up/over）]
例句 他用木板將窗釘上。

- The passengers boarded the plane at 9 a.m.		 vt. （船，車，飛機等）
例句 旅客們上午九時登上飛機。

- She boarded with a Japanese family.		 vi. 伙，包飯；膳宿 [Q]
例句 她寄宿在一個日本人的家裡。

- The helicopter landed with Joe at the controls.		 n. 縱裝置 [P]

53 spread /sprеd/

- I spread my arms as far apart as I could.		 vt. 伸展，使延伸

- Mom spread a new table-cloth on the table.		 vt. 開；展開；攤開

- She was told not to spread this secret around.		 vt. ；散布，傳播；普及
例句 她被告知不要把這個祕密傳出去。

- A wide stretch of land spreads in front of us.		 vi. 展，延伸；延長 [（+out）]

- The fire spread from the factory to the warehouse nearby.		 vi. 開；蔓延
例句 火從工廠蔓延到了附近的倉庫。

- The wings of the bird have a 12-inch spread.		 n. 展；擴張；範圍；寬度 [S]
例句 這鳥的翼展有十二英寸。

- The spread of pests damaged countless fruit trees.		 n. 延；流行；普及 [the S]
例句 蟲害的蔓延損害了無數果樹。

- The spread of the prairies was indeed a startling sight.		 n. 廣闊的) 一片區域 [C]
例句 草原的浩瀚確是奇觀。
