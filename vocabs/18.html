# Title Definition
181 council | 美 ˈkaʊnsḷ | /ˈkaunsil/

- They will hold a council to discuss the proposal.		 n. 議；政務會；協調會 [C] [G]
例句 他們將開會討論這個建議。

- He is in council now.		 n. 事，商討 [U]
例句 他正在議事。
182 local | 美 ˈlokḷ | /ˈlǝukǝl/

- He works at the local post office.
- Local conditions must be taken into account in mapping out the plan.		 a. 1 地方性的；當地的，本地的
例句 他在本地的郵局工作。
例句 在制訂計畫時必須考慮當地的情況。

- The doctor thinks local anesthesia will do for the operation.		 a. 部的
例句 醫生認為這次手術用局部麻醉就可以了。

- Only the local train stops at my station.		 a. 美國英語 】 (公共交通工具) 每站停的
例句 我那個車站只有普通列車會停。

- One of the locals showed me the way to the post office.		 n. 地居民，本地人 [P]
例句 一位本地人給我指點去郵局的路。

188 still /stil/

- Her grandmother is still living.
- They are still working in the fields.		 ad. 1 還，仍舊
例句 她祖母仍然健在。
例句 他們還在田裡工作。

- He is good-natured, but I still don't like him.		 ad. 管如此，然而；(雖然……) 還是

- It's hot today, but it'll be still hotter tomorrow.
- The sick child looks still worse today than yesterday.		 ad. 強調比較級) 還要，更

- The little girl kept still in her chair watching TV.		 a. 止的，不動的 [F]

- The lake is still today.		 a. 靜的；靜默的；平靜的
例句 今天湖水十分平靜。

- The local government did everything in its power to still the storm.		 vt. 【書面語】 使平靜；使靜止，止住
例句 當地政府盡一切努力平息這場風潮。

- Someone stole into his room in the still of night.
- He is good-natured, still I don't like him.		 n. 書面語】 寂靜 [the S] [（+of）]
例句 夜深人靜時有人偷偷溜進了他的房間。 conj.
例句 他脾氣很好，可是我仍然不喜歡他。
