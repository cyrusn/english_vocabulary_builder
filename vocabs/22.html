228 strike /straik/

- He was struck by lightning.
- She struck him with a stick.		 vt. ，擊，攻擊 [O1]
例句 他被閃電擊中。
例句 她用棍子打他。

- The snake struck the man's hand.		 vt. 用爪) 抓傷；(用毒牙) 咬傷；(疾病) 侵襲
例句 蛇咬傷了那男人的手。

- The clock struck twelve.		 vt. 鐘) 敲響報（時）
例句 鐘敲十二點。

- His knee struck against the chair.
- The enemy struck at dawn.		 vi. ，擊；襲擊；進攻 [（+out）]
例句 他的膝蓋撞到了椅子上。

- Twelve has just struck.		 vi. 鐘) 敲，鳴；(時間) 被鳴報

- He struck on a solution to the problem.		 vi. 然想到；突然發現 [（+on/upon）]

- The commander decided to make an air strike on the enemy's supply line.		 n. 擊；攻擊；空襲
