- He was kind and affectionate, but very serious.		 a. 肅的，莊嚴的

例句 我晚餐時說的話是當真的。

- Ann and Mary maintained their friendship for the next thirty years.		 vt. 持；保持；使繼續

- Frank maintains his car very well.		 vt. 修，保養

- He maintains that he once saw a UFO.		 vt. 持；主張；斷言 [+（that）]

- They permitted her to leave.
